# KeyEquations: Electronic Materials - Lecture 10

## Crystal Structure Equations

**Lattice parameter for FCC:**
$$a = \frac{4R}{sqrt{2}} = 2Rsqrt{2}$$

**Atomic packing factor (APF):**
$$APF = frac{text{Volume of atoms in unit cell}}{text{Volume of unit cell}}$$

For FCC: $APF = 0.74$ (74%)

**Atomic concentration:**
$$n_{at} = frac{x}{a^3}$$

## Vacancy Concentration

**Equilibrium vacancy concentration:**
$$n_v = N expleft(-frac{E_v}{kT}right)$$

Where:
- $n_v$ = vacancy concentration (atoms/cm³)
- $N$ = total atomic sites (atoms/cm³)
- $E_v$ = vacancy formation energy (eV)
- $k$ = Boltzmann constant ($8.617 times 10^{-5}$ eV/K)
- $T$ = absolute temperature (K)

## Miller Indices

**Miller indices from intercepts:**
$$text{Intercepts: } x_1, y_1, z_1 rightarrow text{Miller indices: } left(frac{1}{x_1}, frac{1}{y_1}, frac{1}{z_1}right)$$

## Planar Concentration (FCC)

$$n_{100} = frac{2}{a^2}$$

$$n_{110} = frac{2}{a^2sqrt{2}}$$

$$n_{111} = frac{4}{sqrt{3}a^2}$$

## Molecular Orbitals (H₂)

**Bonding orbital:**
$$psi_sigma = psi_{1s}(r_A) + psi_{1s}(r_B)$$

**Antibonding orbital:**
$$psi_sigma^* = psi_{1s}(r_A) - psi_{1s}(r_B)$$

## Energy Bands

**Number of states in band from N atomic orbitals:**
$$2N text{ states (spin up + spin down)}$$

**Band half-filling:**
$$text{If N electrons per N atoms } rightarrow text{band is } frac{1}{2} text{ full}$$

## Fermi-Dirac Related

**Photoemission:**
$$KE = hf - Phi$$

Where:
- $KE$ = kinetic energy of emitted electron
- $hf$ = photon energy
- $Phi$ = work function (eV)
