# Test2: Atomic Structure and the Mole

## Conceptual Questions

1. Distinguish between atomic number (Z), atomic mass number (A), and atomic mass.

2. What is the difference between ionization energy and electron affinity? Which process is always endothermic and which can be either exothermic or endothermic?

3. Why is the atomic mass unit defined relative to carbon-12 rather than hydrogen?

## Calculation Problems

1. **Atomic Composition**: An element has Z = 26 (iron) and A = 56. How many protons, neutrons, and electrons does a neutral iron atom have?

2. **Mole Calculation**: Calculate the number of atoms in 56 grams of iron (Fe).
   
   Given: $N_A = 6.022 \times 10^{23}$ mol$^{-1}$

3. **Weight to Atomic Fraction Conversion**: A Pb-Sn solder contains 38.1 wt% Pb and 61.9 wt% Sn.
   
   Atomic masses: $M_{Pb} = 207.2$ g/mol, $M_{Sn} = 118.7$ g/mol
   
   Calculate the atomic fraction of Pb in the solder.

4. **Reverse Conversion**: If the atomic fraction of Sn in the above solder is 0.739, confirm that the weight fraction calculation yields approximately 61.9% Sn.

## Answers

1. **Atomic Composition**:
   - Protons: Z = 26
   - Neutrons: A - Z = 56 - 26 = 30
   - Electrons: 26 (neutral atom)

2. **Mole Calculation**:
   $$n = \frac{m}{M} = \frac{56 \text{ g}}{56 \text{ g/mol}} = 1 \text{ mol}$$
   $$N = n \times N_A = 1 \text{ mol} \times 6.022 \times 10^{23} \text{ mol}^{-1} = 6.022 \times 10^{23} \text{ atoms}$$

3. **Weight to Atomic Fraction**:
   $$n_{Pb} = \frac{38.1/207.2}{38.1/207.2 + 61.9/118.7} = \frac{0.1838}{0.1838 + 0.5215} = \frac{0.1838}{0.7053} = 0.261$$

   Atomic fraction of Pb = **0.261** (or 26.1 at%)
   Therefore, atomic fraction of Sn = **0.739** (since n_Pb + n_Sn = 1)

4. **Reverse Conversion Check**:
   If n_Sn = 0.739, then n_Pb = 0.261
   
   $$w_{Sn} = \frac{0.739 \times 118.7}{0.739 \times 118.7 + 0.261 \times 207.2} = \frac{87.7}{87.7 + 54.1} = \frac{87.7}{141.8} = 0.618 \approx 61.9\%$$

   The calculation confirms the original weight fraction of 61.9% Sn.
