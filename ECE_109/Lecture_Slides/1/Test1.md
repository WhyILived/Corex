## Conceptual Questions

1. What is the difference between wavelength and wavenumber?

2. In the wave equation $E(z,t) = E_0\cos(kz - \omega t + \phi_0)$, what does each term represent: $E_0$, $k$, $\omega$, $\phi_0$?

3. State two wave properties of light that demonstrate its wave nature.

## Calculation Problems

1. Light with a frequency of $5 \times 10^{14}$ Hz travels through vacuum. Calculate:
   - The angular frequency $\omega$
   - The wavelength $\lambda$

2. A wave has a period of $2 \times 10^{-15}$ s. What is its frequency and angular frequency?

3. Show that the phase velocity $v = \omega/k$ has units of m/s.

## Answers

1.
   - $\omega = 2\pi f = 2\pi(5 \times 10^{14}) = 3.14 \times 10^{15}$ rad/s
   - $\lambda = c/f = (3 \times 10^8)/(5 \times 10^{14}) = 6 \times 10^{-7}$ m = 600 nm

2.
   - $f = 1/T = 1/(2 \times 10^{-15}) = 5 \times 10^{14}$ Hz
   - $\omega = 2\pi f = 2\pi(5 \times 10^{14}) = 3.14 \times 10^{15}$ rad/s

3. $\omega$ has units rad/s, $k$ has units rad/m, so $\omega/k = (rad/s)/(rad/m) = m/s$
